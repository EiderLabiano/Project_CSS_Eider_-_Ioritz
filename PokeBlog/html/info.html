About Pokéblog

Pokéblog is a non-profit webpage designed to connect people with the same interests. pokémon from around the globe. It provides a wide selection of tools to express your post with your personality, such as a profile picture, favorite Pokemon display, image and link support and more. Also, other users can like, dislike, or comment. about your post, which can be interacted with too. The webpage also provides other services, such as a news page, with news submitted by news organizations and users alike, a pokédex page with updated information about all researched pokémon of the world, and an account system that you can fully personalize. All of this is managed. by the passion of a small group of people.
